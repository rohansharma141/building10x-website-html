Questions

Things people ask.

How much does a project cost?
Websites for small businesses typically start around ₹20,000, and a website-plus-growth package around ₹45,000. Larger or custom builds (platforms, commerce, AI tools) are scoped individually. You'll always get a clear, fixed quote before any work begins — no hourly surprises.
How long does it take?
Much faster than traditional development. Because I build with AI-accelerated engineering, a professional website can go live in days, and even larger builds ship in a fraction of the usual timeline. You'll get a realistic date in the planning step.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes. I work with clients across the UK, EU, Middle East, US, Canada and Australia, as well as locally in Delhi NCR. Most work happens remotely, with clear communication throughout.
I'm not technical. Can you still help?
Absolutely — most clients aren't technical, and that's the point. You explain your business and your goal in plain language; I handle everything technical and explain things without jargon. You work directly with me, not a call centre.
What's the difference between automation and AI?
Automation removes effort — repetitive tasks happen by themselves (data syncing, alerts, follow-ups). AI adds capability — things you couldn't do before (intelligent agents, content at scale, prediction). Most businesses benefit from both; I'll help you figure out which solves your problem.
Do you offer ongoing support?
Yes. After launch you can take a support or improvement plan, or just reach out when you need changes. Your site and systems are yours — I'm here when you want to grow them further.
